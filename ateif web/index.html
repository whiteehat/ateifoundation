Who We Are

ATEIF exists to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity.

Adedoye Tech & Entrepreneurship Ignite Foundation is an impact-driven organization focused on helping students, young entrepreneurs and underserved communities access practical knowledge, mentorship and innovation opportunities.

We believe talent is everywhere, but access is not. Through training, community programs, school outreach, conferences, digital literacy projects and partnerships, ATEIF helps people move from raw ideas to structured, bankable and scalable outcomes.

Vision

To become a leading African foundation raising future-ready innovators, entrepreneurs and ethical leaders.

Mission

To equip young people and communities with practical technology and entrepreneurship skills for sustainable growth.
