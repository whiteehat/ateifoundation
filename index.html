About ATEIF

Adedoye Tech & Entrepreneurship Ignite Foundation is an impact-driven organization bridging the gap between talent and opportunity through education, mentorship, innovation and enterprise development.

Vision

To become a leading African foundation raising future-ready innovators and entrepreneurs.

Mission

To equip communities with practical technology and entrepreneurship skills for sustainable growth.
